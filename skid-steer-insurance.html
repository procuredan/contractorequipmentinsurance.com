Why Skid Steer Insurance Matters

Skid steers are one of the most commonly stolen and frequently damaged pieces of contractor equipment. Whether used for excavation, grading, landscaping, snow removal, utility work, or site preparation, skid steers are exposed to major risks every day.

Contractor equipment insurance and inland marine coverage can help protect skid steers from theft, collision damage, rollover incidents, vandalism, fires, transport damage, and certain weather-related losses.

Many contractors also need coverage while transporting skid steers between jobsites or while temporarily storing equipment overnight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does skid steer insurance cover theft?

Many contractor equipment insurance policies can provide protection against theft depending on policy structure and selected coverages.

Is skid steer insurance required?

Many lenders, leasing companies, and jobsite contracts require contractors to carry equipment coverage.

Can skid steers be insured while transported?

Coverage may apply during transport depending on the policy and coverage options selected.

What is inland marine insurance?

Inland marine insurance helps protect contractor equipment and tools while away from a fixed business location.

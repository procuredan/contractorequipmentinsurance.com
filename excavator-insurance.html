Excavator Insurance Coverage

Excavators are some of the most expensive and important pieces of contractor equipment used by excavation companies, utility contractors, site preparation crews, and grading contractors.

Contractor equipment insurance and inland marine coverage can help protect excavators from theft, accidental damage, rollover incidents, vandalism, fire losses, and transportation-related claims.

Because excavators are often transported between jobsites and temporarily stored in unsecured locations, contractors face significant financial exposure if equipment is damaged or stolen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does excavator insurance cover theft?

Many contractor equipment insurance policies can help protect excavators against theft depending on selected coverage options.

Can excavators be insured while transported?

Coverage may apply while transporting excavators between jobsites depending on policy structure and terms.

What type of insurance covers excavators?

Many contractors use inland marine insurance and contractor equipment coverage to protect excavators and heavy machinery.

Is excavator insurance required?

Lenders, leasing companies, and certain project contracts often require equipment insurance coverage.

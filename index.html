Common Questions

Contractor equipment insurance FAQ

What is inland marine / contractor equipment insurance?

It's coverage that protects contractor tools and equipment while in use, in storage, or being transported away from a fixed location — exposures standard property policies often exclude.

Does it cover theft and rented equipment?

Depending on how the policy is structured, coverage can include theft and vandalism, and many contractors add protection for rented or leased equipment. We review exactly what your policy does and doesn't cover.

Is equipment covered while being transported?

Transit coverage can apply while equipment is being hauled between jobsites, but it's a common gap. We check whether your in-transport exposure is actually covered.

Is there any cost or obligation?

No. The review is free, there's no obligation to switch, and your information is never sold or shared.
